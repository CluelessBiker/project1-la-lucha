About us


Athens first FoodTruck delivering delicious, affordable, gourmet Mexican food to those who are willing to seek it out.

“La Lucha” meaning “The Fight” in Spanish, was dreamt up with one thing in mind. We wanted to inspire people to find their appetites and revel in the marvel of tastes & textures. Eating should not simply be a tool to sustain our bodies, but an inspiration to our souls. And good food brings people together.

With this simple & yet elegant set up, we invite you to join us on our journey. Seek us out. Say hello. And become a part of the La Lucha family.
